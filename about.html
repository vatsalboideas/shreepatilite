About

Physician, Surgeon, Researcher, and Healthcare Entrepreneur

Dr. Pallavi Pandey is a physician, surgeon, researcher, and healthcare entrepreneur with a vision to transform the future of healthcare through innovation, precision medicine, and patient-centered solutions. As the CEO of Shreepati Pharma and Healthcare Limited, she combines clinical expertise, global research experience, and entrepreneurial leadership to build a next-generation healthcare enterprise.

After earning her MBBS and Master's degree in General Surgery in Maharashtra, India, Dr. Pandey pursued advanced research opportunities in the United States. In 2016, she joined Johns Hopkins hospital, Baltimore, Maryland, as a Post-Doctoral Research Fellow in Body and Cardiothoracic Imaging, collaborating with fellow and other researchers at Johns Hopkins University and its Biomedical Engineering Department. Her work focused on advancing medical imaging technologies and their application in clinical practice.

In 2024, she joined Massachusetts General Hospital and Harvard Medical School as part of the Tumor Imaging Metrics Core Program, where she has contributed to the evaluation of more than 1,500 oncology cases using RECIST 1.1 criteria for whole-body tumor assessment.
